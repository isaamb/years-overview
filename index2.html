AP Prep

To prepare for the AP CS Exam, I started with following the video review series made by Ajay Gandecha (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AnTD7U_zGkg&list=PLfpeXtDSa8rUl8S9NtbPKlg0ikc-rw-LC&index=2). At the end of each unit, there would be a practice test that I could take that included multiple choice and free response questions. I did this for each unit and tracked how I did on each quiz to see what I understood and what I needed to go back and review. After I went through every unit, I took a practice exam, and ended up not doing as well as expected based on my review (close to a 55% on multiple choice, for context). I realized that the videos missed out on a lot of the details since the videos were meant to be review. I did some practice FRQs and studied more for the things I missed. Ultimately, I got into Georgia Tech and later found out I would not get credit for the AP CSA exam for my major and thus ultimately decided to not take the exam to better focus on physics and calc (that I can get credit for), but now I think I’m well prepared for any Java beginnings next year!
